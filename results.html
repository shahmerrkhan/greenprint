Understanding your footprint

🍽️ Food emissions

Based on lifecycle assessment data from the Oxford University "Reducing food's environmental impacts" study (Poore & Nemecek, 2018). Emission factors per kg of food, averaged across production methods.

🚗 Transport

Vehicle emission factors from Environment and Climate Change Canada (ECCC) national inventory data. EV grid-average emissions based on provincial grid intensity from the Canada Energy Regulator.

✈️ Aviation

Flight emission factors from the UK Department for Energy Security and Net Zero (DESNZ) 2023 conversion factors, including radiative forcing multiplier for high-altitude emissions.

🏠 Home energy

Heating and electricity emission factors from ECCC National Inventory Report. Home size baselines from Statistics Canada housing data (2021 Census).

🌍 Country averages

Per-capita national averages from Our World in Data, sourced from the Global Carbon Project and IEA. Canada average: 15.4t CO₂e/yr per capita (2022).

📍 Local resources

Resource locations pulled live from OpenStreetMap via the Overpass API. Data is community-maintained and may not be fully complete for all areas.
